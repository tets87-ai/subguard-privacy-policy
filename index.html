Privacy Policy

App: SubGuard
Effective date: 2026-01-29

Overview

SubGuard is a privacy-first subscription tracking app. The app is designed to work offline and does not require an account. We do not sell personal data and we do not show ads.

Data We Collect

We do not collect or transmit your subscription data.

  • Subscription names, amounts, renewal dates, and notes are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not run analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or telemetry in the app.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, or advertising identifiers.

Permissions

  • Notifications (Android 13+): used only to show local renewal reminders you schedule in the app.
  • Exact alarms (where supported): used only to improve reliability of scheduled reminders.
  • File access / clipboard (when you use import): used only to import subscription data you explicitly provide.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide app features on your device, such as subscription tracking, insights, and local renewal notifications.

Third-Party Services

SubGuard does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising services. Your data is not shared with third parties.

Data Retention

Data remains on your device until you delete it inside the app or uninstall the app.

Security

We use platform-provided storage mechanisms. No method of storage is 100% secure, but SubGuard is built to avoid transmitting your data off-device.

Children’s Privacy

SubGuard is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date above and publish the updated version at this same URL.
